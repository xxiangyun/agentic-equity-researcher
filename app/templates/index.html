Run an agentic equity researcher on one company.

Use this page to assemble a public-source research draft with KPI snapshot, peer context, valuation view, and source links before you refine the investment view.

What it does

Creates a research draft, not a final recommendation.

Best for liquid US names where public filings, market data, and recent news are easy to verify quickly.

What you should expect

Fast first pass, transparent sources.

The output is strongest when you treat it as a starting packet: check the sources, tighten the thesis, and improve the note.

Agent 1

Source Scout

Pulls price action, recent coverage, and public market context for the company.

Agent 2

Filing Tracker

Maps the ticker to SEC coverage and gathers recent filing activity for the packet.

Agent 3

Document Reader

Reads filing text and pulls snippet-level evidence from the primary SEC documents.

Agent 4

Peer Mapper

Builds a comparable set from your peer list and sector context.

Agent 5

Note Writer

Assembles the research draft, KPI table, valuation view, and linked sources.
